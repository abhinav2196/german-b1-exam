## üó£Ô∏è **Essential Pronunciation Rules**

### **1. Vowel Sounds (Critical!)**
- **a** = "ah" as in "father" ‚Üí **Tag** (day)
- **e** = "eh" as in "bed" ‚Üí **Wetter** (weather)
- **i** = "ee" as in "see" ‚Üí **mit** (with)
- **o** = "oh" as in "boat" ‚Üí **Sohn** (son)
- **u** = "oo" as in "boot" ‚Üí **Uhr** (clock)

### **2. Consonant Rules**
- **ch** = soft "sh" after e,i OR hard "kh" after a,o,u
  - soft: **ich** (I), **sprechen** (to speak)
  - hard: **acht** (eight), **Buch** (book)
- **sch** = "sh" sound ‚Üí **Schule** (school)
- **sp** at beginning = "shp" ‚Üí **sprechen** (to speak)
- **st** at beginning = "sht" ‚Üí **Stadt** (city)
- **w** = "v" sound ‚Üí **wir** (we)
- **v** = "f" sound ‚Üí **vier** (four)
- **z** = "ts" sound ‚Üí **Zeit** (time)

### **3. Word Stress**
- **Usually on first syllable:** LEH-ren (to learn), AR-bei-ten (to work)
- **Compound words:** First part stressed ‚Üí DEUTSCH-land, KLAS-sen-zim-mer
